AICTE-LITE PROGRAM

AICTE’s Leadership in Teaching Excellence (LITE) is a national programme to select and equip 50 colleges and 50 schools with leadership in teaching excellence.

PUPILFIRST

Pupilfirst is a founding member of National Educational Alliance for Technology (NEAT) programme of the Ministry of Education, that is implemented by AICTE. Pupilfirst’s mission is to enable teachers to transform classroom education to a learner centered model and achieve near 100% learning outcomes.

Pupilfirst's Vision

Our vision for the classroom of tomorrow is to create a “Learner Centered Model” with high quality learning outcomes where nearly all students achieve learning objectives for the course. Through Connected Learning pedagogy, students choose between Self Learning, Peer Learning and Teacher assisted learning to suit their personal learning styles.
